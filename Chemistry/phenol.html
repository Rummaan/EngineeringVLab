Phenol Formaldehyde


5ml Glacial Acetic Acid

2.5ml of 40% Formaldehyde

2ml Phenol

Conc HCL

Glass Rod

Final product


Procedure

1. Take 5ml glacial acetic acid in a beaker
2. Add 2.5 ml of 40% formaldehyde solution
3. Add 2ml of phenol to the solution
4. Add a few ml of concentrated HCl into the mixture
5. Stir the solution with the glass rod
6. A large mass of pink plastic (Phenol Formaldehyde Resin) is produced.
